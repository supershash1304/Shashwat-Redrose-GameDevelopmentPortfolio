Seasons Of Serenity

3d Cozy Styled RPG

Highlights

  • Seasons of Serenity is a 3d single player cozy styled adventure game where Neel (player/character) takes upon the role of a “Weather Changer” who can switch between four seasons-Spring, Summer, Monsoon, and Winter to interact with the environment, the world will be shown as a series of different biomes inspired by games like “Tiny glade” and “Cozy grove” , each season dynamically alters the environment, enabling new paths ,the game has both cozy exploration with strategy boss combat. The final boss(Heet) features new programmed technique (2d matrix randomizer) and 4 mini bosses programmed with FSM/BT, the gameplay will be relaxing but will be quite engaging as there will be boss and NPC battles.
  • Implemented systems: input, camera, UI, and save/load.
  • Built internal tools to accelerate content iteration.
  • Shipped on PC/Console with performance budgets met.
  • Currently under development
